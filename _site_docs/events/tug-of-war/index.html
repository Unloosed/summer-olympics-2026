Physical Challenges

Tug-of-War

A classic test of strength and teamwork.

Duration: 45 minutes
Location: Back Lawn
Format: Double Elimination
Type: Team-based
Experience: All levels welcome
Capacity: 8 teams

Description

Heave-ho! Bring your friends and family together for this legendary display of power.

Rules

  1. Teams must have equal numbers of participants.
  2. No wrapping the rope around your body.
  3. The first team to pull the center marker over the line wins.

Requirements & Equipment

Gloves (recommended)
