Grandmaster Chess

A battle of wits and strategy on the 64-square board.

Duration: 2-3 hours
Location: The Library
Format: Single Elimination
Type: Individual
Experience: Intermediate recommended
Capacity: 16

Description

Chess is the ultimate game of strategy. Can you outthink your opponents and claim the title of Grandmaster?

Rules

  1. Standard FIDE rules apply unless otherwise specified.
  2. Each player has 15 minutes on their clock (Rapid format).
  3. "Touch move" rule is in effect.

Requirements & Equipment

None (Boards and clocks provided)
