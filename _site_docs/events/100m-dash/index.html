Sports

100m Dash

A classic sprint to determine the fastest participant.

Duration: 30 minutes
Location: Main Track
Format: Heats and Final
Type: Individual
Experience: All levels welcome
Capacity: 24

Description

The 100m dash is all about explosive power and speed. Whether you're a seasoned runner or just looking for some fun, this event is a highlight of the games!

Rules

  1. Participants must stay in their assigned lanes.
  2. A false start will result in a warning; a second false start by the same participant results in disqualification.
  3. The finish is determined by the participant's torso crossing the finish line.

Requirements & Equipment

Running shoes (optional), comfortable athletic wear.
